About Us

Book Sansar is more than a bookstore, it's a hub for readers and learners. Established in Bharapur-7, Buddhachowk, our mission is to make books accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Vision

  • Promote a strong reading culture in Nepal
  • Provide affordable access to books of all genres
  • Blend traditional library services with modern online shopping

Why Choose Us

  • Vast collection across genres
  • Comfortable reading space
  • Online platform for browsing and purchasing
  • Friendly staff and community-driven events
